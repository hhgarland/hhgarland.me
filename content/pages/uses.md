## Hardware

- Motherboard: Gigabyte Z97-HD3
- CPU: Intel Core i5-4440
- GPU 1: Intel Xeon E3-1200 v3/4th Gen Core Processor Integrated Graphics Controller
- GPU 2: AMD Radeon RX 460 Graphics
- Memory: 16 GiB
